San Francisco 01 Jun 2013

There are about six square miles in San Francisco that seem to have a particularly high density of tech startups. Most startups seem to be concentrated in SoMa, though many of them also seem to be located in the Mission district and Potrero Hill. Many of these companies have one-word, two-syllable cutesy names and simple logos, and they do something related to mobile and/or advertising. This is the “hip” section of the tech world in many ways.

Music Libraries 28 May 2013

With iTunes, Spotify, YouTube, etc., it’s easier than ever to find that one artist, one album, one song that only you connect with. You don’t have to sift into shelves of vinyl or go through a ton of CD racks at some random record store; you just need to type something into a search bar. The fact that it’s becoming so easy to be “unique” though means that our music tastes are just another bit making up our digital fingerprints.
